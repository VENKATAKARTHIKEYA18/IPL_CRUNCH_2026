Surprising Real-Data Finding

Winning the toss provides NO statistically proven advantage (p=0.26). The 51.62% win rate is indistinguishable from a coin flip. However, the decision after the toss is hugely significant — fielding first wins 54.77% vs batting first at only 45.43%.

Strategic Recommendation

Always choose to field first when you win the toss. The data shows batting first after winning the toss is actually worse than the 50% baseline. Field first and chase — it's the IPL-proven formula.
